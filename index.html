Who are We

About Us

At RefreshHub, we specialise in vending solutions designed to enhance convenience and satisfaction across workplaces and events in Greater Sydney. Our comprehensive service covers every aspect of vending machine operations, including procurement, delivery, installation, and daily management; all at no cost to your organisation. We work closely with each client to provide customised snack, drink, and coffee options tailored specifically to your team's preferences and needs.

Why Choose Us

Our Differentiator

  1. Completely Free Setup and Service: Our end-to-end vending solutions come at no initial cost to your business, with no hidden charges or ongoing service fees.
  2. Fully Managed Operations: We handle every detail, from machine procurement and installation to restocking and routine maintenance, ensuring hassle-free convenience for you.
  3. Personalized Product Selection: Collaborate with us to choose snacks, beverages, and coffee varieties that perfectly match your team's tastes and dietary preferences.
  4. Reliable and Efficient Technology: Our smart vending machines use remote monitoring for inventory management, enabling timely restocking and minimal downtime.
  5. Rapid Customer Support: We provide prompt, reliable technical assistance and maintenance, typically resolving any issues within 24 hours.
  6. Local Expertise, Broad Reach: While our core operations focus on Greater Sydney, we have the capacity to serve organisations throughout Australia, maintaining consistent service quality wherever you are.
